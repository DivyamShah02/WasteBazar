Revolutionizing Scrap Trading in India

Founded in 2019, WasteBazar emerged from a simple vision: to create a transparent, efficient, and accessible platform where scrap traders could connect, share information, and grow their businesses together.

We recognized that the traditional scrap trading industry lacked a centralized information hub. Buyers struggled to find reliable sellers, and sellers couldn't reach their ideal customers. WasteBazar bridges this gap by providing a comprehensive platform for information sharing and direct connections.
